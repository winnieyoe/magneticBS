Confronting Counternarratives

Since August 8, 2019, the Hong Kong Police Force has been conducting daily press-briefings at 4pm to address events pertaining to the Anti-Extradition Amendment Bill protest.
Magnetic Bullshit stems out of the interest in using computational approaches to address my mental and emotional inability to process pro-establishment narratives.

Methodology

I scraped the auto captions from 120+ Hong Kong police press conference videos, which were uploaded on YouTube by Chinse state-owned English language news channels with an English simultaneous interpretation, to generated a 627-page transcript. The most frequently used 300+ words are then used as source text for the magnets.

The visual format is a reference to the popular fridge poetry kit Magnetic Poetry. You can create your interpretation of the police’s narrative, reveal your version of truth, or even “receive" an apology.
