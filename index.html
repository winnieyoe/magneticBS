Magnetic Bullshit: Hong Kong Police Edition

Confronting Counternarratives

Since August 8, 2019, the Hong Kong Police Force has been conducting daily press-briefings at 4pm to address events pertaining to the Anti-Extradition Amendment Bill protest.
Magnetic Bullshit stems out of the interest in using computational approaches to address my mental and emotional inability to process pro-establishment narratives.

Methodology

I scraped over 120 Hong Kong police press conference YouTube video, uploaded with an English simultaneous interpretation by Chinse state-owned English language news channel, to generate a 627-page transcript.

The most popular 300+ words are then used as source text for this project.
